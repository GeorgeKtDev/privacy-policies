Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fantasy Roll ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Fantasy Roll application for Apple Watch.

1. Information We Collect

  • App data: Fantasy Roll does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data, including your settings, preferences, and roll history, is stored locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.
  • Accounts: Fantasy Roll does not require an account and does not collect names, emails, or profile information.

2. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

Fantasy Roll uses RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. While we do not collect personal data ourselves, RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data as required by Apple to complete purchases. This data is handled according to RevenueCat's privacy policy and Apple's App Store policies.

  • We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Any data collected by RevenueCat is handled under their respective policies.

3. Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, device information, or any other metrics.

4. Data Storage

We do not operate a backend server for Fantasy Roll. The app does not upload your data or store your personal information on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Fantasy Roll does not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
